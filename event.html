WALKATHON ON DE-ADDICTION

To promote the de-addiction campaign , Ahmedabad Institute of Technology Engineering College situated at gota organised a walkathon on 17 th February on S,G Highway. The main motive behind the rally was spreading the awareness in the society about the adverse effect of injurious addictions .Tobacco, alcohol and dangerous drug addictions are not only persistent in India but it has resulted into a widespread issue all over the world, for which governments are also opening de-addiction centers. Hence its very necessary to spread the awareness among Indian youth and various strata of society to be aware about the disadvantage of addictions and motivate them to live a healthy life for better future.

KITE FESTIVAL

Ahmedabad is often called the Kite capital of Gujarat. Every Year AIT organizes kite carnival for students as well as staff. Students enthusiastically took part in the annual kite festival organized by the AIT. However, the number of kites flying high in the sky increased considerably within a few minutes. While a majority of students were able to quickly fly their kites, a few were struggling to get their kites released from the thread.







CULTURAL FEST

A cultural function adds to the flavor and taste of life and makes it worth-living. The Cultural Committee of AIT is committed for holistic development of students, by creating an environment where talents are nurtured every year. The committee conducted various programmes throughout the year and brings out the vibrant colours of extracurricular activities. That is why, every student looks forward to it with keen interest.Whole the function was echoed with clapping. Everybody enjoyed the cultural function and highly appreciated the organizers.
