MECHANICAL ENGINEERING

One of the oldest and broadest engineering branch, Mechanical Engineering involves designing, development, production and use of mechanical tools to solve mechanical issues. Generally hired by manufacturing industries, these engineers have employment opportunity in both private and public sectors. Government sectors like Defense, PWD, Post and Telegraphs, and CPWD also hire mechanical engineers. Ahmedabad Institute Of Technology’s Department of Mechanical Engineering was established in 2004, which offer “Bachelor of Engineering”. The department runs one post graduate “Master of Engineering in” since 2012 under Gujarat Technological University. In house We Have Lots of Club like ISHARE , CADCLUB which Makes the student industry Ready.
